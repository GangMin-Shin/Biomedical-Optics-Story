Mach-Zehnder Interferometer Simulation

This interactive Mach-Zehnder interferometer simulation shows how the optical path difference, wavelength, fringe spacing, and visibility affect the interference pattern observed at the detector.

Detector intensity model:
I = I0[1 + V cos(Δφ)] / 2
Δφ = 2πΔL / λ

Detector Interference Pattern

The bright and dark bands represent constructive and destructive interference. When ΔL changes, the phase difference changes, so the fringe pattern shifts. When visibility decreases, the contrast between bright and dark fringes becomes weaker.
