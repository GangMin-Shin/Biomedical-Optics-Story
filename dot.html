Diffuse Optical Tomography (DOT)

Diffuse Optical Tomography (DOT) is an optical imaging method that reconstructs tissue optical properties from multiply scattered near-infrared light.

This page summarizes DOT forward modeling, banana-shaped sensitivity functions, inverse problems, and reconstruction concepts used in biomedical optics.
