MMF–DSCA Optical Design

Laser source 808 nm near-infrared light source for diffuse optical flow measurement.
50:50 fiber beam splitter Splits the source light into multiple illumination paths.
Source fiber Delivers coherent near-infrared light to the tissue surface.
Sensitivity volume Banana-shaped photon sampling region between the source and detector.
Detector fiber Collects dynamically scattered light from the tissue.
Fan-out fiber bundle Transfers detected speckle patterns to separated camera regions.
Lens system and camera Images the fiber outputs onto the camera sensor for speckle analysis.
ROI selection Selects the region of interest for each detected speckle image.
BFI / BVI calculation BFI is derived from speckle contrast fluctuation, while BVI is derived from intensity-based optical density.

Microvascular Simulation

BFI BFI means Blood Flow Index. In this simplified simulation, faster RBC motion represents stronger flow-related speckle fluctuation.
