Sagnac Interferometer Simulation

The Sagnac interferometer splits one laser beam into clockwise and counter-clockwise beams that travel around the same closed optical loop. When the loop rotates, the two beams accumulate a relative phase shift. This page visualizes how rotation rate, loop area, wavelength, visibility, and phase offset change the detector intensity and interference fringe pattern.
