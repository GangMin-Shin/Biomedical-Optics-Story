The sensitivity map is calculated by a browser-based Monte Carlo random-walk model. First, an axisymmetric R-Z fluence map is estimated from a surface source. Then the source and detector fluence fields are shifted according to SDS, and the final sensitivity is calculated as W = Φs × Φd.
